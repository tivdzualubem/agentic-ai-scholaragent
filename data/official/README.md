# Official-source development corpus

This directory contains a small development corpus constructed from official
scholarship webpages saved under `data/sources/official/`.

It is intended to validate ingestion, retrieval, eligibility screening,
grounding, citation verification, and abstention behavior. It is not a
comprehensive scholarship database and must not be treated as the final
research benchmark.

Scholarship requirements and deadlines can change. Every recommendation must
therefore retain its official URL, source-check date, manual-review conditions,
and provenance entry from `source_manifest.json`.
